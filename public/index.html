Find a place to eat right now.

1 - Survey Restaurants

Send an alert to nearby restaurants. Specify how far you want to travel, when you want to eat, and how many people are in your party.

2 - Receive Offers

Restaurants respond in real-time with their current availability and any specials. Offers remain valid for ten minutes, so respond quickly!

3 - Choose a Restaurant

Accept an offer and eat! An accepted offer binds both the restaurant and you. Remember to mention Pronto when you arrive to receive your special.

ABOUT

Pronto was borne from the fustration of not knowing where you can eat right now. Rather than calling restaurants individually, have those with availability contact you. Even better, you may discover a nearby hidden gem!

Pronto is not a substitute for reservations (when you know where you want to eat ahead of time). It does, however, answer the three common questions before most meals:

  1. What is open right now?
  2. Do they have space for my group?
  3. Are there specials I should know about?

Pronto represents a two-week student project by Hack Reactor students Jimmy Tsao , Joe Dou , and Jeffrey Gho . Technologies used include MEAN (MongoDB, Express, Angular, and Node) and the mobile framework Ionic.
